HOW TO PLAY: Move with the arrow keys or with WASD. There are signs that will tell you important stuff.

Level Creator: Click to create blocks.
Append "?levelCreator=true" at the end of the URL to activate.
Keymaps:
Example keymap
[t]: Color
This means that pressing the t key will change your mouse power mode to color. This is an example and is NOT real.
[1]: Normal
[2]: Sticky
[3]: Portal
[4]: Lava
[5]: Ice
[6]: Bounce
[7]: Mud
[8]: Sign
[9]: Blocker
[0]: Cubie
[-]: Water
[=]: Sand
[[]: Monster Green
[]]: Monster Blue
[\]: Set Spawnpoint (WARNING: THE SPAWNPOINT IS NOT SAVED)
[;]: Loose Sand (Sand spawns as individual blocks)
Press [B] for savecode
Press [C] for level creator
Press [V] to clear blocks in level creator
Press [G] to change cubie gravity
Press [H] to change block gravity
Press [P] pause/unpause
Press [Z] undo
Press [X] to toggle debug
Press [T] to toggle between all the texture packs
When you press [B], a savecode will pop up. Copy that and email it to maitiansha@gmail.com with the subject as [Level Name] by [Who created the level] and the savecode in the main form.
